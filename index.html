How to Play Old Maid!

Old Maid is a simple card game. The objective is to avoid being the player left with the unmatched card, known as the Old Maid.

1. All players are dealt an equal number of cards. One card, usually a Queen, is removed to create the Old Maid.
2. Players remove pairs of matching cards from their hand.
3. Players take turns drawing a card from the next player's hand.
4. If the drawn card forms a pair with another card in hand, the pair is removed.
5. The game continues until all pairs are removed and only the Old Maid remains.
